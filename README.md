# 👗 Wardrobe Wars

> A fashion rating web app built as a first-year group project at the University of Manchester.

Upload your outfit, rate it, and let the wardrobe speak for itself.

---

## 📸 About the Project

Wardrobe Wars is a lightweight, browser-based app that lets users upload photos of their outfits and rate them using a 5-star system. The concept came from wanting a fun, visual way to get feedback on your style — no accounts, no backend, just you and your fits.

The app was designed with a dark, dramatic aesthetic — deep navy backgrounds, spotlight effects, and hot pink accents — to give outfits the stage they deserve.

---

## ✨ Features

- 📤 **Upload any outfit photo** directly from your device
- ⭐ **Interactive star rating** — click to rate from 1 to 5 stars
- 🗑️ **Delete & reset** — remove the outfit and clear the rating in one click
- 👤 **Profile page** — see your stats (outfits uploaded, ratings given, average score) and a personal outfit gallery
- ⚙️ **Settings page** — manage your display name, username, and preferences like public ratings and notifications

## 🚀 Getting Started

No installation, no dependencies, no build step — it runs entirely in the browser.

1. **Clone or download** this repository
2. Open `home.html` in any modern browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge)
3. Click **Upload** to add an outfit photo
4. Rate it using the ★ stars
5. Hit **Delete** to clear and start fresh

---

## 🎨 Design Decisions

- **Dark theme** with a deep navy-to-purple gradient background to make outfit colours pop
- **Pink (`#ff2d78`) as the accent colour** for interactive elements like stars, buttons, and active nav links
- **Spotlight graphic in the background** to simulate a fashion show runway feel
- **Glassmorphism-style buttons** using semi-transparent backgrounds and blur effects
- The layout is intentionally minimal — the outfit photo is the hero of the page
